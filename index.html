I’m Ysabel Wang.

I am a Master’s in Oceanography student at Texas A&M University. My thesis investigates the formation and fate of coastal dense water masses.

I am interested in coastal and observational oceanography and autonomous and unmanned instrumentation. At the Geochemical & Environmental Research Group, I work on the university’s Liquid Robotics WaveGlider.

On this website, you’ll find my most recent CV and some projects and code.
